Intro

Since beginning my journey in this field, my dedication to the work and motivation to grow have led me to exceptional projects and experiences.

Take a moment to explore my portfolio below, which includes a combination of collaborations, early prototypes and Finished projects. Don’t hesitate to reach out if you’d like to learn more.

Battle Heros

A Firstperson Shooter Game (PVP - Multiplayer), Where the player who gets the highest kill count in a match wins.

Project Description

Genre : Action, Shooter

Target Platform : Android

Role : Level Designer

My Work

As a level designer, I focused on creating multiplayer levels for Battle Heroes. The game requires the map to have spawn points for 10 players, and the level design revolves around that. I also participated in playtesting to ensure that everything was working as intended.

Dew Drops

Dew has been falling from high in the sky to reach the Earth's surface. Clear the incoming obstacles and help the drop reach its destination.

Project Description

Genre : Hyper-Casual, Puzzle

Target Platform : Android

Role : Level Designer

My Work

This is a pitch made for a publisher. By the time it reached me, the game designs were already completed, and my senior shared his vision, assigning me to design a level for the game. Initially, the gameplay was simple, but as development progressed, my team realized the game would work better as a puzzle. So, the simple gameplay was transformed into a puzzle.

Harpoon Master

Want to destroy stuff? Throw your harpoon, hook it, and pull it back for satisfying destruction. If anything gets in the way, you'll pull it along too.

Project Description

Genre : Casual, Puzzle

Target Platform : Android

Role : Level Designer

My Work

This is another pitch made for a publisher. To create the intended experience, we needed to simulate a specific role. We tested various assets, selected one, and built the game around it. During development, we encountered multiple bugs related to the physics simulation, but we eventually made the game playable with 15 levels. I was assigned as the team leader for this project, where I stayed focused on the game's vision, guided my fellow developers, and helped bring the game to life.

RiddleMath

Want to test your calculating skills? Look no further than RiddleMath! It’s a fun and engaging cross-math puzzle game that’s quick and easy to play anytime, anywhere.

Project Description

Genre : Puzzle

Target Platform : Android

Role : Level Designer

My Work

This is the first game in which I used a custom level generator tool to quickly create cross-levels. As a level designer, I applied my basic mathematical skills to develop these levels. The tool makes my work much easier and allows more time to playtest my creations. The game is live on the Google Play.

Triple Tile Match 3D

Immerse yourself in the world of match-3 puzzle games, where you can match cute objects and relax with Triple Tile Match 3D.

Project Description

Genre : Puzzle

Target Platform : Android

Role : Level Designer

My Work

This is one of the client projects I worked on at Knifecake Studios, where we were tasked with improving the game. Our plan was to enhance the visuals and add new levels. As a level designer, my goal was to create a compelling puzzle experience by introducing difficulty spikes at certain intervals to challenge players, while experimenting with time and in-game objectives. The game has been updated on the Play Store, and real-world testing is currently in progress.
